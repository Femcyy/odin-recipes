Spaghetti Boursin

Description

In this recipe we are going to make Spaghetti Boursin with pieces of bacon. It is very easy to make and will only take 15 minutes. Only 3 ingredients are needed. Therefore, it is the ideal student meal to make when you do not have the time!

Furthermore, the only tools that are needed are two pans. One to boil the spaghetti, and another to fry the bacon. Let's get started!

Ingredients

    200 g Bacon
    300 g Spaghetti
    1 package of frozen Spinach Boursin

Steps

    Boil some water, add salt and cook the spaghetti al dente in 12 minutes.
    Put the spinach boursin in a pan on low heat in order to defrost the spinach.
    Fry the pieces of bacon.
    Add the pieces of bacon to the defrosted spinach.
    Strain the spaghetti, and add the spaghetti to the spinach bacon sauce
    Enjoy!
