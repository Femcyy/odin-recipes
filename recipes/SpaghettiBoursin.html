Spaghetti Boursin

Description

In this recipe we are going to make Spaghetti Boursin with pieces of bacon. It is very easy to make and will only take 15 minutes. Only 3 ingredients are needed. Therefore, it is the ideal student meal to make when you do not have the time!

Furthermore, the only tools that are needed are two pans. One to boil the spaghetti, and another to fry the bacon. Let's get started!
